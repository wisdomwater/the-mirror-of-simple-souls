## Day 92 — Only One Will

> When you glimpse how completely Love has given itself, something in you begins to fall silent.

You behold the poverty he chose, the shame he endured, the mortal body he entered—for you. Not as an idea, but as a tenderness willingly exposed. In that sight, the mind cannot contain what the heart begins to sense. One benefit alone—one act of such love—outweighs all destruction, all loss, all fear.

You may notice how this vision purifies your seeing. Not by striving to become spotless, but by drawing you toward a likeness. Only what reflects the Son can rise into that clarity. Not through effort, but through grace that slowly reshapes you from within, until your life begins to echo his.

And why was all this done? Not from necessity. Not from pressure. Love tells you it was for one reason only: because the divine will willed it. Because Love desired to love.

There are many holy images that steady the heart—the apostles aflame, Mary consenting, John crying in the wilderness, the seraphim resting in fire. Yet beneath them all is one simple beholding: the divine will moving in freedom, unforced, unbound.

When you see this, something loosens. Your own restless willing softens. You begin to sense him in you, and yourself in him. Your great desires quiet, not because they are denied, but because they are gathered into a deeper current.

And you may find yourself whispering: I do not know what You are. I do not know what I am.

But I am held within Your will.

**Breath Prayer**

> In: *Your will is love*
>
> Out: *I rest inside it*
