## Day 91 — Where Love Overflows

> Where Love overflows without measure, it loosens your grip on yourself.

Consider the abundance given to Mary—even before she could speak or choose. Filled with light in the hidden place of her mother’s womb, she was made spacious enough to bear the One who would mend what Adam marred. At the Cross she stood, not recoiling from cruelty, not bargaining for vengeance, but consenting to mercy that poured wider than the wound.

The re-making of the world did not require a measured payment. Even the smallest drop would have been enough. Yet Love did not give sparingly. Love gave in excess—lavish, aching, unguarded. Not because it was required, but because it is Love’s nature to overflow.

The divine took on our tenderness and our vulnerability, not from compulsion, but from union. Love bound itself to our humanity so completely that it would not turn away from our sorrow. Who would have dared to ask for such a thing? And yet it was given freely.

Sometimes a thought of this comes quietly to you—a glimpse of mercy greater than your self-concern. If you let it, that thought begins to unfasten you from the tight hold of your own efforts. You may notice how often you have tried to secure yourself through works and reasoning, only to find yourself weary.

Love does not restore you by measure. It restores you by gift.

And perhaps you are being invited to let that gift work more deeply than you have yet allowed.

**Breath Prayer**

> In: *Love overflows toward me*
>
> Out: *I release myself to You*
