## Day 89 — Above the Law, Within Love

> *When the will is gone, love alone remains to move the soul.*

Truth speaks gently over the soul who has fallen into fine love. She is called emerald, diamond, queen—not because she has achieved greatness, but because she has ceased asking for anything but the pleasure of her Beloved. She no longer seeks love’s riches. She desires only what delights Him.

And then she says something astonishing: “I am not.”

It is not despair. It is release.

Love has taken her so fully into its keeping that she claims no separate wit, no private will, no guarded reason. Whatever moves in her moves for Him alone. She is not emptied into absence, but into belonging.

To those who live by outward rule and visible effort, this freedom may seem dangerous. Yet she is not against the law. She is beyond it in the way fruit is beyond the seed. The law once guided her steps; now love fulfills what the law intended. She does not discard goodness—she lives from its source.

There are secrets in this union that cannot be spoken to every ear. Some are governed by fear, some by calculation, some by desire still negotiating with love. Even heaven seems to whisper to her, gently, to keep silence.

For honey and wine cannot be explained.

They can only be tasted.

**Breath Prayer**

> In: *Hold me wholly in You*
>
> Out: *Let love be my only will*
