## Day 100 — Where Love Remains

> When love has finished its quiet work, nothing remains between the soul and the Beloved.

There comes a gentle ripening in the soul when the long labor of seeking begins to fade. What once felt like a journey—full of questions, efforts, and turning—grows still. Love has slowly surrounded the soul on every side until there is nowhere left to stand apart.

At first you may think you are loving God. Later you begin to see that Love has been loving you long before your own heart awakened. And in time even this distinction softens. Love receives love. Love answers love. The movement between the two becomes so simple that it can scarcely be named.

Nothing is lost in this union, and yet much quietly falls away. The anxious self that tried to secure its place grows transparent. The distance that once seemed to separate lover and Beloved dissolves into a deeper closeness. You remain yourself, and God remains God, yet the bond between you becomes so intimate that the boundary is no longer the center of attention.

It is like two flames leaning into one another. Each keeps its light, yet the fire is shared.

Here the soul no longer searches for proof, nor measures how near it has come. Love has carried it beyond such concerns. What remains is a quiet belonging, where self and Beloved dwell together without striving to define the space between them.

And in that stillness, Love continues loving.

**Breath Prayer**

> In: *Love holds us together*
>
> Out: *Nothing stands between us*
