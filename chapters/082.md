## Day 82 — Joy Beyond Reason

> *There is a joy that rises where reason cannot follow.*

When love has undone you enough, something simple begins to happen. The Beloved gives Himself without reserve, not to display power, but to show that all being flows from Him alone. And in that giving, your willing and His willing grow quiet together, like two streams becoming one current.

Sometimes this soul rejoices without even feeling the joy.

She is lifted inwardly, drawn toward a gate where her will rests inside His. She remembers mercy—how she has been carried, forgiven, gathered—and that remembering becomes light. Not a light she produces, but one that bears her upward into union. There she delights in His delight. The good of others becomes her gladness. The salvation of her neighbor is her pleasure.

Yet Reason stirs and protests.

It sees tears in the world and cannot understand this hidden joy. It fears presumption. It calls such gladness blindness. But Reason cannot see what has no end, for it lives by what it can measure and conclude.

Love sees differently.

It knows that divine goodness must be poured somewhere. And the soul, emptied of her own claims, becomes a place wide enough to receive it. Not because she is worthy, but because she is needy. Her poverty becomes the open door through which mercy flows.

And in that exchange—her lack and His abundance—joy blooms beyond explanation.

**Breath Prayer**

> In: *Pour Your goodness here*
>
> Out: *I open in my need*
